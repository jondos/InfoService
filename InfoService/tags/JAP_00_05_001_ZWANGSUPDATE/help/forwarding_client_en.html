Forwarding Client

Forwarding Client Settings

  • I need the help of a forwarder to contact the anonymity service.
    Select this checkbox if you cannot reach the IP address of the anonymity service because that IP address is blocked by a firewall, by your Internet Service Provider, or by your government.
    When this option is activated, you can use the connections of other JAP users to gain access to the anonymity service.

  • I'm also not able to reach an InfoService directly.
    Select this checkbox if the IP address of the InfoService is also blocked.
