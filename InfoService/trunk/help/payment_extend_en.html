Payment: Advanced Settings

In this dialog you may specify advanced settings for payment.

  • Show confirm dialog before using a service with costs
    Activating this option will cause JAP to display a confirmation dialog each time a service with costs is about to be used. This also means that JAP will not be able to connect to a service with costs automatically during startup.

  • Allow non-anonymous connections to payment instances
    It is recommended to activate this option. Thereby JAP can get information about your account balance even if you have no anonymized connection currently established.
    If this option is deactivated, account information may be gathered through anonymized connections only.

  • Automatically update account balance
    If activated, JAP will try to update your account balance at fixed intervals.
