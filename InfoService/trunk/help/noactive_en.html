Avoid data trails on the internet

Websites could offer content that will reveal your identity even when surfing anonymously with JAP. Examples for such contents are JavaScript, Java and Cookies. Sometimes browsers offer comfortable options for blocking such content, sometimes the usage of additional programs is to be considered.
In order to manage your cookies in a sensible way, consider using CookieCooker (Windows only). Descriptions and HowTos can also be found at this page. For further information about cookies, please read this.
