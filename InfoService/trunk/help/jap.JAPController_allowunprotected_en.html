Surfing without anonymity mode

JAP prevents you from unintentionally surfing without being connected to any anonymiser service. Although, you have the possibility to allow this explicitly.

By default, JAP will ask you again for a choice after a few seconds. If you choose to remember your answer, it will be saved and JAP won't ask you again. If you have decided to allow non-anonymous surfing, JAPP will never remember your answer permanently, as this would be a potential security risk four you. Your answer is only remembered until you reconnect to any anonymity service or you restart JAP.
