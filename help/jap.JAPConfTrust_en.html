Trust

This panel is currently being developed and will be noticeably extended.

To get to this panel, press button Settings in JAP's main window, then choose the entry Trust from the list to the left. You may also click on the button Edit in the main window.

These settings define the criteria for the trust that you give to anonymizer services. The possible values are:

  • Not trustworthy: For you, this is an excludinbg criterium for a trustworthy service/Mix.
  • Hardly trustworthy: You do not know exactly if you should trust a service/Mix with this attribute.
  • Trustworthy: A service/Mix with this attribute may be trustworthy for you.
  • Exclusively trustworthy: A service/Mix must have this attribute to be trustworthy for you. (not available for all atrributes)
  • Payment services
    The trust you give to services with costs.

  • Expired certificates
    The trust you give to a Mix with at least one expired certificate in its verification chain.
